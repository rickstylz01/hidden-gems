Hidden Gems uses Google Autocorrect to predict user inquiries.

Type in the search bar and choose your selection with either the click of your mouse or, you can use the arrow keys to select and the tab key to confirm your selection.

Hidden Gems is a project created with the Google Local Context Beta API. It uses the Google Maps and Places API to locate businesses, POI's, restaurant's and the like.

I created Hidden Gems to give light to the less known places to eat within your local community or area. There have been many times where I've found a "hidden gem" of a restaurant that isn't overly advertised or popularly spoken about. Many times these restaurants, cafe's, etc. go mostly unnoticed except for the locals who stop by to just get a bite to eat and end up discovering deliciously awesome food. Now you too can enter your location and discover what hidden gems, your local area has to offer.
